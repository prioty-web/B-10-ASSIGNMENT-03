Question often asked!!!

What is your fee structure and what is included?

The designer should provide a clear explanation of their fee structure, whether it's a flat fee, hourly rate, or percentage of the project cost. Ensure they outline what is included in their fees (e.g., design, revisions, site visits) and any additional costs that might arise.

Can you provide examples of projects similar to mine?

A good designer should have a structured process for managing both design and construction phases. This includes details on how they coordinate with contractors, handle permits, and ensure that the design is executed as planned. They should also be transparent about their involvement throughout the project.

How do you handle changes or revisions during the project?

The designer should have a well-defined process for managing changes, including how they handle revisions to the design, any associated costs, and how these changes affect the project timeline. This ensures that you know how flexible they are and what to expect if changes are needed
